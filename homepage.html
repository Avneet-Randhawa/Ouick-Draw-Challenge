Welcome To Quick Draw Game


In Quick Draw Game, You will be given with some objects to draw within the timer on the canvas and You have to draw them.Every correct drawing will increment your score. If time passes and the object drawn by you is not correct then your will lose your turn and you will be given with other object to draw. For Better understanding
To Start The game click on Normal Game Play OR Custom Game Play button to start the game.
An alert will be displayed on the top of the game, Press Okay.
Your Game will Be Started.

    Difference Between Normal Game Play and Custom Game Play

  • Normal Game Play : In this game, you cannot change the numbers of total number of objects you have to draw and time given to the player.
  • Custom Game Play : In this game, you can change the numbers of total number of objects you have to draw and time given to the player.
